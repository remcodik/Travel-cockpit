# Design Rules

Travel Cockpit follows these principles.

- Simplicity over complexity.
- Maps before lists.
- AI assists, never decides.
- Accommodation is the operational centre of every travel day.
- Google Maps remains the primary navigation solution.
- Offline support is a core feature.
- The traveller always remains in control.
