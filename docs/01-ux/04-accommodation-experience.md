# Accommodation Experience

**Document ID:** TC-UX-004  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Owner:** Product Team

---

# Purpose

The accommodation is the operational base of every travel day.

Everything the traveller wants to do starts from the active accommodation.

The accommodation screen is therefore one of the most important screens in the application.

---

# Philosophy

Travellers naturally think:

"I'm staying here today."

The application should therefore answer:

"What can I do from here?"

---

# Header

Display:

- Accommodation photo
- Accommodation name
- Country
- Region
- Arrival date
- Departure date
- Number of nights
- Distance from current location

---

# Quick Actions

Always available:

- Google Maps
- Call
- Website
- Booking
- AI
- Share

---

# AI Suggestions

The traveller can ask:

- What can I do today?
- Suggest a scenic drive.
- Suggest a short walk.
- Find a good café.
- Find a restaurant.
- Suggest rain alternatives.
- Refresh suggestions.

AI suggestions are never added automatically.

---

# Nearby

Display nearby:

- Activities
- Restaurants
- Cafés
- Energy Points
- Parking
- Supermarkets

The radius can be adjusted.

---

# Planning

Display only activities that belong to this accommodation.

Examples:

Today

Tomorrow

Completed

Ideas remain separate.

---

# Accommodation Information

Store:

- Address
- GPS coordinates
- Phone
- Website
- Booking link
- Check-in
- Check-out
- Notes

---

# Personal Notes

Examples:

- Gate code
- Parking instructions
- WiFi password
- Contact person

All notes remain offline.

---

# Photos

The traveller may add personal photos.

These replace internet images if preferred.

---

# Travel Companions

Display:

- Shared with
- Last synchronisation
- Offline status

---

# Dynamic Behaviour

Arrival Day

Focus on:

- Check-in
- Parking
- Nearby restaurants

Stay

Focus on:

- Activities
- Restaurants
- AI

Departure

Focus on:

- Check-out
- Next accommodation
- Route
- Charging/Fuel

---

# Success

The traveller should be able to spend an entire day using only this screen as the starting point.
