# Discover

**Document ID:** TC-UX-005

**Project:** Travel Cockpit

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Draft

**Owner:** Product Team

---

# Purpose

Discover helps travellers find interesting places around their current accommodation or current location.

Unlike Planning, Discover only contains ideas.

Nothing in Discover is automatically added to the trip.

The traveller always decides.

---

# Philosophy

Travel Cockpit should inspire.

It should never overwhelm.

Discover presents a curated selection of relevant places instead of endless lists.

---

# Starting Point

Discover can search from:

- Active Accommodation (default)
- Current Location
- Selected Place
- Map Area

The active starting point is always clearly visible.

---

# Sources

Discover combines results from:

- AI suggestions
- Saved ideas
- Nearby places
- Personal favourites
- Previous trips (future version)

---

# Categories

The traveller can browse:

- Activities
- Restaurants
- Cafés
- Scenic viewpoints
- Waterfalls
- Museums
- Beaches
- Walking routes
- Scenic drives
- Supermarkets
- Parking
- Charging stations
- Fuel stations

---

# Filters

Available filters include:

- Distance
- Category
- Difficulty
- Duration
- Open now
- Free
- Paid
- Family friendly
- Dog friendly
- Rain alternative
- Accessible

The application remembers the last selected filters.

---

# AI Suggestions

The traveller may ask AI:

- What should we do today?
- Surprise me.
- Show hidden gems.
- Find something nearby.
- Refresh suggestions.
- Compare these two activities.

AI suggestions remain temporary until accepted.

---

# Place Card

Each Place is displayed as a card.

The card contains:

- Name
- Photo
- Distance
- Category
- Short description
- Estimated duration
- Google Maps
- Add to Planning

---

# Refresh

Refresh replaces the current suggestions with new ones.

The traveller can refresh repeatedly.

Previously rejected suggestions are temporarily avoided where possible.

---

# Save for Later

Interesting places can be saved without adding them to Planning.

Saved places remain available throughout the trip.

---

# Offline

Previously loaded Discover results remain available offline.

New searches require an internet connection.

---

# Design Principle

Discover should always answer:

"What would be a good idea right now?"

The traveller should feel inspired without losing overview.
