# Product Blueprint

**Project:** Travel Cockpit  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Approved  
**Owner:** Product Team

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the vision, principles and functional foundation of Travel Cockpit.

Every design and development decision must align with this document.

If a new feature conflicts with the Product Blueprint, the blueprint takes precedence.

---

# 2. Vision

Travel Cockpit is a travel companion designed to support travellers during their journey.

Unlike traditional travel planners, the application focuses on helping users make decisions while travelling instead of planning every detail beforehand.

The application combines maps, accommodations, activities, restaurants, cafés, energy points, tickets and AI into one central cockpit.

Travel Cockpit does not replace specialised applications.

It connects them.

---

# 3. Mission

Help travellers spend less time searching and more time enjoying their journey.

Everything should be available from one place.

---

# 4. Product Philosophy

Travel Cockpit is based on five principles.

## Simple

Every function should reduce complexity.

---

## Practical

Only information that is useful during the trip belongs in the application.

---

## Fast

Important actions should never be more than one tap away.

---

## Flexible

Travellers decide during the journey.

The application supports this instead of enforcing schedules.

---

## User Controlled

The traveller always stays in control.

AI advises.

Users decide.

---

# 5. Target Audience

Travel Cockpit is designed for people who:

- travel by car
- visit multiple accommodations
- choose activities during the trip
- appreciate visual maps
- want AI assistance
- prefer flexibility over strict planning

---

# 6. Core Principles

### Accommodation is the starting point

Every day starts from the active accommodation.

---

### Maps are central

The map is one of the main screens.

---

### Google Maps remains the navigator

Travel Cockpit launches navigation.

It does not replace navigation.

---

### AI supports

AI never plans automatically.

AI never changes data automatically.

AI only provides suggestions.

---

### Offline first

Essential travel information remains available without internet.

---

### One tap to action

Important actions should require only one tap.

---

# 7. Success

The application is successful when travellers naturally open Travel Cockpit before opening any other travel-related application.

---

# 8. Scope (Version 1)

Included:

- Trips
- Accommodations
- Activities
- Restaurants
- Cafés
- Energy Points
- Tickets
- Maps
- Google Maps
- Offline support
- AI Assistant
- Shared Trips
- Personal Photos
- Roadtrip Mode

---

# 9. Out of Scope

Not included:

- Social media
- Reviews
- Booking accommodation
- Navigation engine
- Automatic planning
- Cost administration
- Gamification
- Chat

---

# 10. Design Rule

Every new feature must answer one question:

> Does this make travelling easier while being on the road?

If the answer is **no**, the feature does not belong in Travel Cockpit.
