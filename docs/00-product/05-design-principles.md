# Design Principles

**Document ID:** TC-PROD-005  
**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Stable

## Purpose

These principles guide every product, UX and technical decision within Travel Cockpit.

---

## 1. Traveller First

Every feature must create value for the traveller.

---

## 2. Roadtrip First

The application is designed primarily for multi-day road trips.

---

## 3. One Place Model

Every physical location is represented by a Place.

Accommodation, restaurants, viewpoints and charging stations are specialised Place types.

---

## 4. AI Assists

Artificial Intelligence supports the traveller.

AI never makes decisions on behalf of the traveller.

---

## 5. Offline First

Essential travel information remains available without an internet connection.

---

## 6. Simplicity

Simple solutions are preferred over feature-rich but complex solutions.

---

## 7. Current Context

The application continuously adapts to the active trip, accommodation, traveller and current location.

---

## 8. Future Proof

The architecture should allow future expansion without redesigning the core concepts.

---

## Guiding Principle

> Build less. Build better.
