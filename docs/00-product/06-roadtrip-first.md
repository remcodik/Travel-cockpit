# Roadtrip First

**Document ID:** TC-PROD-006  
**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Stable

## Purpose

Travel Cockpit is designed primarily for multi-day road trips by car.

Every design decision should first support the road trip experience before considering other travel scenarios.

## Characteristics

Road trips typically include:

- Multiple accommodations
- Flexible daily planning
- Scenic routes
- Frequent stops
- Discovering places along the way
- Weather-dependent decisions
- Charging or refuelling
- Offline situations

## Design Implications

The application should:

- Make changing plans easy
- Show nearby opportunities
- Use the active accommodation as the operational centre
- Adapt recommendations to the current context
- Keep navigation simple
- Minimise repetitive data entry

## Guiding Principle

> The journey is as important as the destination.
