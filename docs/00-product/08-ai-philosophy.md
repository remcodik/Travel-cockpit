# AI Philosophy

**Document ID:** TC-PROD-008  
**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Stable

## Purpose

Artificial Intelligence is an assistant within Travel Cockpit.

Its role is to reduce effort, provide insight and help travellers make better informed decisions.

## AI Responsibilities

AI may:

- Explain options
- Compare places
- Suggest activities
- Recommend alternatives
- Summarise information
- Answer travel questions

AI never:

- Changes planning automatically
- Makes bookings
- Deletes traveller data
- Overrides traveller choices

## Principles

- Human remains in control
- Suggestions must be transparent
- Recommendations should use the current context
- AI should improve the travel experience without adding complexity

## Guiding Principle

> AI supports every journey, but the traveller always decides.
