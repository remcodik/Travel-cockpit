# Traveller Profile

**Document ID:** TC-PROD-012  
**Version:** 0.2  
**Status:** Stable

## Purpose

The Traveller Profile stores long-term preferences that personalise every trip.

Unlike the Trip Profile, this information is reused across future journeys.

## Profile Categories

### Personal Preferences
- Preferred travel style
- Pace of travelling
- Photography interest
- Scenic routes
- Local culture

### Activities
- Hiking
- Walking
- Museums
- Viewpoints
- Waterfalls
- Scenic drives

### Accommodation
- Preferred accommodation types
- Budget indication
- Required facilities

### Food & Drink
- Dietary preferences
- Favourite cuisines
- Coffee preference

### Vehicle
- Vehicle type
- Fuel or EV
- Charging preferences
- Ferry preferences

## Principles

- The traveller owns the profile.
- AI may use the profile for recommendations.
- The profile is never changed automatically.

## Related

The Traveller Profile contributes to the Current Context but remains independent of any single trip.
