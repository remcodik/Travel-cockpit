# Purpose

**Document ID:** TC-PROD-001  
**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Stable

## Purpose

Travel Cockpit provides one integrated application that supports travellers throughout the complete lifecycle of a road trip.

Instead of switching between multiple applications for planning, navigation, discovery, weather, charging, notes and travel memories, the traveller uses one coherent environment.

The application combines planning, maps, artificial intelligence and personal travel information into a single experience.

## Primary Objective

Reduce organisational effort and increase the enjoyment of travelling.

## Design Goals

- Personal
- Intuitive
- Reliable
- Offline capable
- AI-assisted
- Roadtrip focused
- Future-proof

## Product Position

Travel Cockpit is not a booking platform, navigation app or travel guide.

It is the central cockpit that connects all travel information and supports the traveller before, during and after every journey.

## Guiding Principle

> Travel Cockpit should become the first application a traveller opens when planning a trip and the last application they close after returning home.
