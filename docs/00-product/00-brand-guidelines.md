# Purpose

This document defines the official identity of the Travel Cockpit project.

The purpose is to ensure consistent naming, branding and communication across documentation, source code and published applications.

---

# Official Product Name

Travel Cockpit

The product name is always written with capital letters:

**Travel Cockpit**

Never use:

- TravelCockpit
- travel cockpit
- Travel cockpit
- Travel Planner
- Travel Companion (as product name)

Travel Companion may only be used as a description.

---

# Product Description

Travel Cockpit is an AI-powered travel companion for road trips.

It combines maps, accommodations, activities, restaurants, cafés, energy points, tickets and intelligent assistance into one central cockpit.

---

# Tagline

**Everything you need for your road trip. One cockpit.**

Alternative (marketing):

**Your AI-powered road trip companion.**

# Naming Principles

The word **Cockpit** reflects the philosophy of the application.

Travel Cockpit is not a planner.

It is the operational centre of the journey.

The application helps travellers make better decisions while travelling without taking control away from them.

---

# Brand Values

Travel Cockpit should always communicate:

- Simplicity
- Reliability
- Freedom
- Exploration
- Practicality
- Intelligence
- Trust

---

# Design Personality

The application should feel:

- calm
- modern
- professional
- premium
- easy to understand

The interface should never feel playful or overloaded.

---

# Logo Direction

The logo should communicate:

- travel
- navigation
- overview
- movement

It should remain simple and recognisable at small sizes.

---

# Colour Philosophy

The application theme may adapt to the destination.

Examples:

Norway

Blue, green and grey.

Italy

Warm terracotta and olive tones.

Scotland

Deep green and blue.

The application should always remain recognisable as Travel Cockpit regardless of the selected destination theme.

---

# Success

Whenever someone opens the application they should immediately recognise:

"This is Travel Cockpit."

The branding should communicate confidence, simplicity and trust.
