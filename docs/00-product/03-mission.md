## Mission

The mission of Travel Cockpit is to help travellers spend less time organising and more time enjoying their journey.

The application supports travellers before, during and after a trip by combining planning, navigation, discovery and personal travel information into one integrated solution.

## How We Achieve This

Travel Cockpit delivers value by:

- Reducing planning effort
- Keeping all travel information together
- Providing relevant AI assistance
- Supporting offline travel
- Adapting to the traveller's context
- Encouraging discovery without overwhelming the user

## AI Mission

Artificial Intelligence supports the traveller by providing explanations, suggestions and comparisons.

AI never replaces the traveller's decisions.

## Mission Statement

> Reduce effort. Increase experiences.
