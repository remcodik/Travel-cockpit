# Product Goal

**Document ID:** TC-PROD-004  
**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Stable

## Product Goal

Travel Cockpit is designed to become the central digital cockpit for road trips.

Its primary goal is to replace the need to switch continuously between different travel applications by bringing all essential travel information into one integrated platform.

## Core Goals

The application enables travellers to:

- Plan complete journeys
- Organise accommodations
- Discover interesting places
- Navigate efficiently
- Keep travel notes
- Store travel memories
- Manage charging or refuelling
- Receive AI assistance
- Continue working offline

## Success

A successful implementation means that travellers can complete an entire road trip while relying primarily on Travel Cockpit.

## Guiding Goal

> Everything a traveller needs. One cockpit.
