# Out of Scope

**Document ID:** TC-PROD-010  
**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Stable

## Purpose

This document defines functionality that is intentionally excluded from the initial releases of Travel Cockpit.

Clearly defining what is out of scope helps maintain focus and prevents unnecessary complexity.

## Out of Scope

The following functionality is not part of the first product releases:

- Hotel booking
- Flight booking
- Restaurant reservations
- Public transport planning
- Social media features
- Automatic trip planning without user approval
- Continuous live location sharing
- Travel agency functionality
- Expense management
- Loyalty programme integration

## Design Principle

Features are only added when they strengthen the core mission of Travel Cockpit.

A smaller, high-quality application is preferred over a feature-rich but complex solution.

## Guiding Principle

> Focus creates quality.
