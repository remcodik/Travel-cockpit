# AI Philosophy

**Document ID:** TC-AI-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Approved  
**Owner:** Product Team

---

# Purpose

This document defines how Artificial Intelligence is used within Travel Cockpit.

AI is a travel assistant.

It is never the travel planner.

---

# Vision

Artificial Intelligence helps travellers discover, compare and decide.

The traveller always remains in control.

AI exists to reduce searching, not to replace decision making.

---

# Core Principles

## AI suggests

AI can:

- suggest activities
- suggest restaurants
- suggest cafés
- suggest viewpoints
- suggest scenic routes
- suggest charging stations
- suggest fuel stations
- suggest rain alternatives

---

## AI explains

Every recommendation should explain why it is recommended.

Examples:

- Beautiful viewpoint
- Popular among hikers
- Suitable in rainy weather
- Only 12 minutes away

---

## AI refreshes

The traveller may request:

Refresh Suggestions

Travel Cockpit generates a completely new set of ideas.

No suggestion is added automatically.

---

## AI completes

AI may enrich existing places with:

- descriptions
- practical information
- estimated duration
- best visiting time
- parking
- difficulty
- nearby cafés

---

## AI compares

Examples:

Compare two hikes.

Compare restaurants.

Compare viewpoints.

Compare charging stations.

---

## AI never

AI never:

- changes planning
- removes activities
- changes accommodation
- changes trip order
- books hotels
- books restaurants
- starts navigation automatically

---

# Confirmation

Every AI action that changes data requires user confirmation.

---

# Context Awareness

AI understands:

- current accommodation
- current trip
- country
- weather
- vehicle type
- completed activities
- planned activities

This context improves suggestions.

---

# Privacy

User data remains private.

AI only receives the information required to answer the request.

---

# Success

The traveller should feel:

"I still make the decisions.

AI simply helps me make better ones."
