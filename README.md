# 🚗 Travel Cockpit

> A smart travel companion for road trips.

Travel Cockpit is a mobile application that helps travellers during their journey instead of only before or after it.

The app combines accommodations, activities, restaurants, cafés, energy points (EV charging & fuel), tickets, maps and AI into one central cockpit.

---

## Vision

Travel Cockpit is designed around one principle:

> Help travellers make better decisions during their trip.

The application does not replace services such as Google Maps, Komoot or Booking.com. Instead, it brings them together in one simple and intuitive interface.
